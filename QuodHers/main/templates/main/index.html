About Us



Why is it important?

Menstruation, physical, emotional and hormonal changes during puberity and in genral female anatomy are the remotely touched and yet censored topics of our society. It is the need of the 21st century to empower women to take charge of their well-being.This can be possible by educating them and making them confident enough about their biology.



How are we different?

We at Sahayika aim to help young girls from rural areas with their physical, mental and emotional well being. NGOs, individuals and other communities can donate, organize training sessions for teenage girls educating them about female anatomy, mestruation, and overall well-being. We act as a direct medium of empowerment between the ones who offer help and the young girls.
